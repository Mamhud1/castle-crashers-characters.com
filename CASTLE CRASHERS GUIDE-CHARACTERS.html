Castle Crashers guide-characters

What is Castle Crashers?

Castle Crashers is a hack and slash game created by Behemoth and Newgrounds.

The plot

It is about 4 princess s who were kidnapped by the Evil Wizard. The Wizard also took the King s (father of the princess s)gem.
So you fight your way to get the princess s and the gem.

Characters

How to get characters

Almost every character unlocks you a new character after you beat the game with it.
Here are the steps to unlock every character on every version of Castle Crashers (PS3, XBOX 360, PC, Remastered):
*Green Knight- Royal Guard- Saracen
*Red Knight- Skeleton- Bear
*Blue Knight- Industrialist- Fencer
*Orange Knight- Fire Demon- Ninja
*Grey Knight(beat the Barbarian King level)- Stove Face
*Barbarian (beat the Barb Arena)- Beekeeper
*Thief (beat the Thief Arena)- Snakey
*Iceskimo (beat the Icy Arena)- Brute
*Conehead(beat the Volcano Arena)
*Alien(Remastered/PS3/PC-beat the Alien Ship level, XBOX 360-beat level 1-1 in Alien Hominid)
*Hatty(Remastered-buy him in the Insane shop for 1200, PC- own Castle Crashers and Battleblock Theatre on the same Steam account, XBOX 360- obtain one achievement in Battleblock Theatre PS3- buy Can t Stop Crying Pack)
*Pink Knight(Remastered- Starting Knight, XBOX 360/PS3/PC- buy Pink Knight Pack DLC)
*Blacksmith(Remastered- Starting Knight, XBOX 360/PS3/PC- buy Legend Of The Blacksmith Pack)
*Cult Minion(Remastered/PS3/PC- beat Ice Castle on Insane mode, XBOX 360- buy Necromantic Pack DLC)
*Necromancer(Remastered/PS3/PC- beat Industrial Castle on Insane mode, XBOX 360- buy Necromantic Pack DLC)
*Open Faced Grey Kinght(Remastered/PS3/PC- beat Catfish on Insane mode, XBOX 360- buy King Pack DLC)
*King(Remastered/PS3/PC- beat Pipistrello s Cave ON Insane mode, XBOX 360- buy King Pack DLC)
*Peasant(beat Peasant s Arena)-Civillian
